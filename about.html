Who We Are?

Located in the heart of Migori County (40400), we are the bridge between premium international brands and local convenience. We started with a small physical shop and a big dream: to ensure no glass remains empty.

We operate a hybrid model. You can walk into our shop to browse our exclusive selection of wines, whiskeys, and gins, or simply tap a button on this website and have it delivered to your doorstep.

From parties to quiet evenings, we bring the cheers to you.
